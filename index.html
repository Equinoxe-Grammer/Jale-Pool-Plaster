About Us

Jale Pool Plaster is a family-owned business with over 15 years of experience in pool and spa renovation, repair, and services in Houston. We specialize in pool plastering, replaster, coping, tile, spray deck, skimmer change, and acid wash. We use the best materials and innovative techniques to ensure durable, high-quality results. Our commitment is to create the perfect oasis for every client, adapting to their needs and style.

Services

Plaster & Replaster

Application and renewal of pool plaster for a fresh and long-lasting look.

Coping

Installation and repair of pool edges to enhance aesthetics and safety.

Tile

Installation and repair of decorative tiles and mosaics.

Spray Deck

Application of non-slip coating for poolside areas.

Skimmer Change

Professional skimmer replacement for better filtration and cleaning.

Cleaning & Acid Wash

Stain and residue removal with professional methods to restore shine.
